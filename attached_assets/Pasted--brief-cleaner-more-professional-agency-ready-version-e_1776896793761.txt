Thikana – Mobile App Product Brief & MVP Scope

1. Project Overview

Product Name: Thikana
Product Type: Mobile application for rental property discovery and listing
Primary Market: Bangladesh, with initial focus on Dhaka
Platforms: Android and iOS
Preferred Approach: Cross-platform development for speed, consistency, and cost efficiency

Thikana is a location-first rental discovery platform designed to help users easily find nearby flats, rooms, sublets, and short-stay accommodations. The product is intended for a crowded and fragmented rental market where available properties are difficult to discover, compare, and contact quickly.

The app should feel simple, fast, trustworthy, and easy to use, especially for local users across different age groups.

⸻

2. Problem Statement

In Dhaka, rental property discovery is highly scattered and inefficient. Users often do not know:

* where rental homes are available,
* which areas currently have options,
* what kind of property fits their needs,
* whether a listing is suitable for family, bachelor, or student use,
* and how to quickly contact the owner or host.

Thikana aims to solve this by combining map-based discovery, area-based browsing, and simple listing creation inside one clean mobile experience.

⸻

3. Product Vision

Thikana will be a clean, location-based rental app where users can:

* discover nearby rental properties on a map,
* browse rental posts by area in a feed format,
* explore a separate short-stay / Airbnb-style section,
* contact owners directly via call or text,
* post their own rental property through a simple flow,
* access customer support when needed,
* and use the platform comfortably through a minimal, modern interface.

The hero experience of the product is map discovery, with posts feed as an equally important secondary browsing mode.

⸻

4. Target Users

A. Rent Seekers

Users searching for:

* family flats,
* bachelor-friendly rentals,
* student-friendly rentals,
* single rooms,
* sublets,
* or other long-term rental options.

B. Property Owners / Landlords

Users who want to:

* create rental listings,
* upload property photos and location,
* and receive calls/messages from interested tenants.

C. Travelers / Short-Stay Guests

Users looking for:

* 1-night stays,
* 2–7 day stays,
* furnished temporary accommodation,
* travel or interview stays,
* and short-duration housing in Dhaka.

D. Admin / Support Team

Internal team members responsible for:

* reviewing and moderating listings,
* handling complaints and reports,
* managing fake or duplicate posts,
* and maintaining support quality.

⸻

5. Core Product Decisions for MVP

The following assumptions should be treated as part of the MVP scope:

1. Default landing screen after splash is the Maps tab.
2. Guest users can browse without logging in.
3. Login is required for posting, saving, and profile/account actions.
4. Phone OTP login is the preferred authentication method.
5. Short-stay in MVP is inquiry-based, not full online booking.
6. Contact methods in MVP can be call, SMS, and/or WhatsApp deep-link.
7. Listings should go through admin review before being published for trust and quality control.

⸻

7. Navigation Structure

The bottom navigation should contain 4 tabs in the following order:

1. Maps
2. Posts
3. Stay
4. More

These are the primary destinations of the application.

⸻

8. Functional Scope

8.1 Maps Tab

This is the main discovery experience of the product.

Objective

Allow users to find nearby rental properties visually through a location-first interface.

Requirements

* Show the user’s current location
* Allow manual search by area/location
* Display listings as:
    * map pins,
    * building icons,
    * or clustered markers in dense areas
* Show the number of listings available in the visible map area
* Tapping a marker should open a small preview card or bottom sheet

Top Section

* Location search
* Filter button
* Optional sort action

Marker Preview Content

* Thumbnail image
* Rent price
* Area/location
* Property type
* Quick summary such as beds, baths, and suitability tags
* CTA to open full details

⸻

8.2 Posts Tab

This is the area-based browsing feed for users who prefer scrolling instead of maps.

Objective

Provide a fast, filterable rental feed in list or grid format.

Requirements

* Card list or grid view
* Infinite scroll / paginated loading
* Area-based rental feed
* Search bar at the top
* Shared filter system with Maps
* Sorting options such as:
    * newest,
    * lowest price,
    * highest price,
    * nearest,
    * most relevant

Listing Card Content

* Cover photo
* Rent price
* Area / location
* Attribute summary such as rooms, baths, balconies
* Tags such as:
    * Family
    * Bachelor
    * Student
    * Furnished
    * Verified (future/admin-controlled)

Add Listing CTA

A prominent floating + button should appear in the bottom-right corner of the Posts screen for creating new listings.

Important Note

Bachelor and Student filters must be clearly visible and easy to use in this section.

⸻

Primary CTA options:

Support Requirement

Every listing detail page must also include:
