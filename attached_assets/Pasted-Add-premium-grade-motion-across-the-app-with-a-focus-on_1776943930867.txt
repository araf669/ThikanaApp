Add premium-grade motion across the app with a focus on buttery smooth, polished, high-end transitions that feel expensive and refined. Animations should feel modern, fluid, and intentional — not flashy, not overloaded, and never laggy. The target experience is a luxury-level app feel with fast, silky, responsive motion, while still keeping implementation efficient and realistic.

Motion direction

The animation system should feel:

* buttery smooth
* premium
* elegant
* responsive
* modern
* soft and refined
* high-end startup quality

The app should not feel static. Every important interaction should have subtle, polished motion.

Core animation requirements

Apply smooth transitions to:

* splash screen logo reveal
* screen-to-screen navigation
* bottom tab switching
* opening filters
* map to detail interactions
* post card tap to detail page
* modal and bottom sheet opening/closing
* button press feedback
* profile/developer info screen entry
* image gallery transitions
* loading states and skeletons

Animation style

Use:

* soft fade
* smooth slide
* subtle scale
* elegant bottom sheet motion
* polished card press interaction
* smooth opacity transitions
* refined easing curves

Avoid:

* harsh bounce
* overly dramatic motion
* slow heavy animations
* jittery transitions
* cheap-feeling default transitions

Performance requirement

Animations must remain smooth and responsive on normal devices. Motion should feel premium without hurting performance. Optimize for fluidity and responsiveness, especially on mobile.

Specific expectations

* tab changes should feel seamless and premium
* cards should have subtle press-in/press-out feedback
* bottom sheets should glide smoothly
* screens should fade/slide in elegantly
* interactive elements should feel alive but controlled
* loading states should look modern and polished
* transitions should give a “million-dollar product” feel

Development direction

Implement a lightweight but premium animation system using efficient mobile-friendly animation practices. Favor smooth native-feeling transitions and reusable motion patterns across the app. Keep the motion design consistent throughout the entire product.

Final objective

The entire app should feel very smooth, very premium, and very expensive in motion quality — like a high-end modern startup app with carefully crafted interactions and buttery transitions throughout.
